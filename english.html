History

It all started in two centuries of war days, when the Crusader Order led the campaign against the old nations. Order prevailed; He did not take captives, he destroyed races one after another. Corpses filled the streets of devastated cities. And in the midst of this putrid stench, the darkest evil came to our world... A terrible curse caused people to go mad and rot alive and gradually turned into ruthless monsters. The cities hit by this epidemic turned into giant hives of those ruled by the will of the herd. Soon all the ancient kingdoms were devoured by the Plague.

Other Information

Our game is a 2D Story adventure game, this project that we have been working on for 2.5 years, has recently reached the Beta Test phase. Game makers: ahmet#1337, tep#2024

Setup

First of all, press the purple download button and download the Setup (Loader). Then open the ZIP file and drag the Plague Hunter Loader.exe inside to the desktop. Then run it and that's it! Complete the setup and enjoy the game. If you find an error or have a suggestion, please do not forget to come to the discord.gg/plaguehunter server and reach us privately. :)
